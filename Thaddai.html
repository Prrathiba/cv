Thattai Recipe

ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 3 tabs flax seed flour
  • 2 tabs psyllium husk
  • 3/4 cup roasted peanut(crushed)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

    • Wet Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 10 red chili (paste)
  • 2 tsp garlic (paste)

    • INSTRUCTIONS

    Mix all dry ingredients together and add all wet ingredients mix it make a big round of ball, and after that make a small size ball out of it...then with tortilla maker press it and put it in a baking sheet with parchment paper and bake it in 350F 10 min after that take out of it and flip it to the other side and after spray coconut oil on top of it...and bake again another 5 more minutes...take out of oven cool for another 5 min Enjoy!!
